TEAM

At Sports Avalanche, we have a dedicated, driven and talented team. Our team is diverse, consisting of a Lawyer, a UEFA Accredited Sports Journalist, Software Developers, Interface Designers and seasoned Business Advisors. We are focused on providing digital solutions to the various problems aspiring soccer/sports professionals and enthusiasts face across Africa and the world at large. We combine in-depth knowledge of the necessary industries and geographies with experience, and passion. We are dedicated to a singular goal; connecting aspiring soccer/sports professionals to global opportunities. We also are determined to augment the scouting process, and introduce stellar innovations to sports globally.
