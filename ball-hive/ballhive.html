Ball Hive

Our Flagship product “BALL HIVE” is an app that lets aspiring football players create profiles, upload content like highlight reels and workout routines, track performances by uploading individual statistics vetted by teammates and coaches, and gives exclusive information about various football trials near them.

Explore Now

The Ball Hive Digital Academy

The start up will have its independent scouts, to fish out talents and periodically enroll them into the Ball Hive Academy. Members of the digital academy will be given access to a 2 week long fellowship in which they will be digitally trained by footballing legends of varying Nationalities, on different areas of footballing in order to enable them understand the various aspects and requirements of having successful football careers. Funding and direct investments will be made on the promising talents to sponsor them to trial opportunities in different locations globally, and the company will get paid percentages from the first professional contracts of the players.

Ball Hive Contest

Periodically, random top performers of different mini tournaments will be given the platform to play against established Football clubs and Academies, coached by randomly selected coaches from our database.

The Ball Hive Monthly Rankings

Every month based off the various activities and strides of users; a Team of the month will be published based on the individual performances viewed by our analysts. The players on the monthly teams of the months will have their profiles and names optimized and imputed in our reports to scouts.
