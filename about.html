About

Who We Are & What We Do

Sports Avalanche Ltd is a Nigerian sports technology startup with a diverse team of Lawyers, Software Developers, Interface Designers, Football scouts and agents, and seasoned Business Advisors. We are focused on providing digital solutions to the various problems’ sports professional face across Africa and the world at large. Our flagship product “Ball Hive” is an app created for football players who are either professionals or aspiring professionals, coaches and scouts. The goal is to facilitate scouting by giving scouts access to a large database of talented players, and give players a platform to track their performance, showcase their talents and access exclusive trials across the globe.

Why We Do It

Thousands of aspiring football professionals do not go pro and are never able to harness their full potential. This is due to a plethora of reasons. First, there is an over reliance on intermediaries because players have no tailor made platform to directly put their talents on display, track their performances and network with people within the footbaq1lling industry. The second problem is the lack of funding, which makes players miss out on trial opportunities globally. This is as a result of the high financial risks involved in aimless travel to chase opportunities without guarantees of fair processes, or prior physical or digital contact with player profiles.
