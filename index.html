Our Programs

We offer a few programs to facilitate your journey to go pro as an aspiring football player, we are doing all it takes to make your dreams come alive.

Digital Academy

Our scouts find talents from the app and enroll them in the Ball Hive Digital Academy. Members of the Academy will be given access to a two-week-long fellowship in which they will be digitally trained by professionals to enable them to understand the various requirements of having successful football careers.

Get Scouted

Sponsorship

Direct investments will be made on the promising talents from the Ball Hive App to sponsor them to trial opportunities in different locations globally. The players will go through the Ball Hive Academy to be groomed by professionals to increase their chances of landing a contract from these trials.

Get Scouted

Rankings & Contest

Compete for a top spot in the monthly player rankings. Players in the Team of the month will have their data included in our reports to scouts to increase their chances. Players from here will be given the platform to play against top football clubs and academies periodically to help them showcase their talents.
